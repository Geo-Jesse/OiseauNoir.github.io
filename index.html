.aboutMe

(Native Coloradoan - living in ATX!)

Hello! I am a full-stack web developer with a background in earth science, project management, and professional music. After a rewarding 9-year career in environmental consulting, I am now pursuing my love of all things driven by data science, interactive design, and the web!

When not coding kickass websites, you may find me running, cycling, melting faces in an Austin divebar, or back in Colorado climbing Fourteeners! Otherwise, I'm home with my girlfriend and our two cats, reading Carl Sagan (#hero).

It is my aspiration to enrich the world through exploration of new ideas, commitment to intellectual integrity, and seeking out Alexandrian solutions to Gordian knots! Above all else, I am a perpetual student of life, with a voracious appetite for knowledge and experience. I have been distinguished throughout my career for the high level of focus and detail I bring to projects, however my greatest accomplishments are the powerful and enduring relationships I have built throughout my career with my clients and colleagues.
